Our story

Six decades. Six showrooms. Same family. Same promise.

Fairdeal International was founded in Kolkata more than sixty years ago — in an era when fans and radios were household essentials, the drawing-room television brought entire neighbourhoods together, and a new refrigerator was something the whole family celebrated.

What started as a single shop in 1966 has grown into six showrooms across South Kolkata — Behala, Tollygunge, Santoshpur, Garia, Sonarpur and Baruipur. The brands we carry have changed. The technology has changed. The one thing that hasn't is the way we sell: tell the customer the truth, give them the right price, and stand behind every product we put in their home.

"The same shop that sold the parents' first TV in the eighties is selling the daughter her first refrigerator today. That's not marketing. That's the business."
